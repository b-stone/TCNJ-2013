Graded Nomenclature

$$R = k[x_1,x_2,\ldots, x_n] = \bigoplus_{i \geq 0} R_i$$ Here $R_i$ is the $k$-vector space generated by monomials of degree $i$.

Elements of $R_i$ are called homogeneous: $$x^2+xy, \ 4xyz^5$$

Ideals generated by homogeneous elements are called homogeneous, and the quotients are graded.
