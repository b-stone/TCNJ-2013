Macaulay's Theorem:

For $h:\mathbb Z_{\geq 0} \to \mathbb Z_{\geq 0}$, TFAE:

  • there exists a graded ideal $I$ in $R$ such that $h_{R/I}(t) = h(t)$ for all $t \geq 0$;

  • there exists a monomial ideal $I$ in $R$ such that $h_{R/I}(t) = h(t)$ for all $t \geq 0$;

  • one has $h(0) = 1$, and $h(t+1) \leq h(t)^{\langle t \rangle}$ for all $t \geq 1$.
